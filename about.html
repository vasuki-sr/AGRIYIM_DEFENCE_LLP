Company Profile

Advancing National Security through Indigenous Marine Engineering and Unmanned Technologies.

About Us

AGRIYIM DEFENCE LLP is an India-based Limited Liability Partnership engaged in the design, manufacturing, and supply of Rigid Inflatable Boats (RIBs) and Unmanned Drone Systems for government and institutional clients.

Operating at the intersection of marine engineering and unmanned technologies, we focus on indigenously manufactured solutions aligned with national requirements, operational conditions, and global quality benchmarks.

Our Objectives

The company has been established with a clear objective to deliver platforms that support maritime security, surveillance, rescue, logistics, and tactical operations:

Mission-Ready

Built for immediate deployment in critical scenarios.

Reliable

Engineered to withstand the harshest maritime environments.

Cost-Effective

Optimized manufacturing without compromising on quality.

Background

Founded by professionals with hands-on exposure to marine operations, boat building, and defence-oriented procurement, Agriym Defence LLP was formed to bridge the gap between operational needs and practical manufacturing execution.

India’s expanding focus on coastal security and technology-driven preparedness has created a strong demand for specialized platforms. We serve this demand through custom-built, application-specific solutions rather than off-the-shelf products.

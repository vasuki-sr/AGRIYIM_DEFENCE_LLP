Our Solutions

Engineering the essential technology for the Digital Ocean—where autonomous robotics, real-time connectivity, and indigenous marine engineering converge.

Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs)

Autonomous Patrol USV

The flagship of our Digital Ocean initiative. These unmanned vessels are designed for persistent maritime surveillance, providing a continuous presence without risking human lives in high-threat or extreme weather environments.

  • Indigenous Navigation: Fully autonomous waypoint following and obstacle avoidance.
  • Long Endurance: Optimized power management for multi-day mission persistence.
  • Modular Payload: Integration for sonar, radar, and 360° optical surveillance.
  • Real-Time Telemetry: Encrypted satellite and RF links for remote command.

USV Swarm Operations

Advanced coordinated control systems that allow multiple Unmanned Surface Vehicles to work in unison. Perfect for large-scale area denial, perimeter security, and complex search operations.

  • Collaborative Intelligence: Vehicles share sensor data for a unified battle picture.
  • Scalable Deployment: Control a fleet of USVs from a single ground station.
  • Dynamic Tasking: Automatically redistributes tasks if a unit leaves the swarm.
  • Tactical Superiority: Overwhelms traditional surveillance with multi-angle coverage.

Subsea Data Gateway

A specialized USV variant acting as a communication bridge between underwater assets (AUVs/Sensors) and global satellite networks, bridging the gap between subsea data and execution.

  • Acoustic-to-RF Conversion: Transmits subsea sensor data to the cloud in real-time.
  • Ruggedized Station Keeping: Maintains position in heavy seas for reliable link-up.
  • Low Signature: Stealth-oriented design to minimize detection during data relay.
  • Solar-Electric Hybrid: Maximizes mission life through renewable energy harvesting.

Unmanned Drone Systems

Tactical ISR Drone Systems

Indigenously manufactured aerial solutions designed for Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance. These systems are custom-built to bridge the gap between operational needs and tactical execution in volatile coastal and border environments.

  • Mission-Ready: Rapid deployment for immediate situational awareness.
  • Reliable: Engineered for extreme maritime and operational conditions.
  • Advanced EO/IR payloads for day and night surveillance.
  • Encrypted data telemetry aligned with national security benchmarks.

Tactical Logistics Drones

Specialized heavy-lift platforms designed for autonomous logistics and supply missions. These systems support the "Digital Ocean" vision by providing cost-effective, unmanned transport to remote maritime locations.

  • Heavy payload capacity for mission-critical cargo.
  • Cost-Effective: Reduces reliance on expensive manned transport.
  • Precision autonomous landing on moving maritime platforms.
  • Fail-safe systems for high-reliability logistics.
